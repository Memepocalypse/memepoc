Famous Meme Coins

Discover some of the most popular meme coins and learn about their history and impact.

Dogecoin (2013)

Launched as a joke, Dogecoin became a cultural phenomenon due to its strong community and meme-based appeal.

Shiba Inu (2020)

Often called the "Dogecoin Killer," Shiba Inu built on the legacy of Dogecoin with its playful branding.

PEPECOIN (2024)

Inspired by Pepe the Frog, this meme coin gained traction through social media as a part of the 2024 meme coin surge.
