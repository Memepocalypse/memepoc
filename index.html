Memepocalypse (MEMEPOC) is a project dedicated to preserving and documenting the cultural evolution of memes. Explore the timeline, learn about famous meme coins, and join a growing community that values meme culture.

The Evolution of Memes

From Ancient Egypt to the 2024 meme coin surge, memes have shaped our culture and continue to evolve with new digital innovations.

Ancient Roman Graffiti (79 CE, Pompeii)

The eruption of Mt. Vesuvius preserved Roman graffiti that mocked politicians and public figures.

Ancient Egyptian Hieroglyphs (c. 3200 BCE)

Depictions of animals in human situations, reflecting an early form of satire.
